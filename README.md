# AI Career Roadmap Generator

## Overview

AI Career Roadmap Generator (ACRG) is a full-stack web application designed to help students and aspiring professionals plan their careers through personalized, AI-driven learning roadmaps. The platform analyzes a user's current skills, educational background, interests, career aspirations, and available learning time to generate a structured pathway toward their target career.

Unlike generic career guidance platforms, ACRG performs skill-gap analysis and creates evidence-based career roadmaps containing recommended skills, learning milestones, projects, assessments, and progress-tracking mechanisms. The system continuously adapts recommendations based on user progress and mentor feedback.

## Problem Statement

Students often rely on scattered online resources, random tutorials, and unstructured learning paths, making career planning difficult and inefficient. Many learners struggle to identify which skills are required for their desired roles, what they should learn first, and how to measure progress.

The AI Career Roadmap Generator addresses this problem by providing a personalized and structured career development framework that guides users from their current skill level to their desired professional role.

## Objectives

- Create detailed student career profiles.
- Identify skill gaps through intelligent analysis.
- Generate personalized career roadmaps.
- Recommend projects and learning resources.
- Track progress and milestone completion.
- Enable mentor review and feedback.
- Provide analytics for career growth measurement.
- Improve career readiness through structured learning plans.

## System Workflow

1. User creates an account and completes their career profile.
2. User selects a target career goal.
3. The system evaluates existing skills and experience.
4. Skill-gap analysis identifies missing competencies.
5. AI generates a customized learning roadmap.
6. The platform recommends projects and assessments.
7. Users track progress through milestones.
8. Mentors review and provide feedback.
9. The roadmap evolves based on completed achievements and updated goals.
